Can Upheal customize a note template for my organization?

Yes, we'll work with your clinical team to produce a note template that matches your requirements.

Is Upheal SOC 2 compliant?

Yes, in addition to HIPAA, PHIPA, PIPEDA, and GDPR, Upheal is SOC 2 compliant. More on our privacy.

Can Upheal be used alongside my telehealth platform?

Yes, we can integrate Upheal seamlessly with your EHR or provider platform. Upheal has a secure and flexible API that supports bi-directional integrations and is part of other EHR integration programs.

Can I assign different roles to my team members?

Yes, we offer different roles in the Upheal app to suit your team members access and administrative needs. Theres no per-seat charge Billing is based on number of sessions, not number of team members, so you can add as many seats as you like.

Is there a free option for teams?

Yes, your team can use Upheal for free. Youll get a 14-day full-featured trial, after which youll move to our Free plan if you dont subscribe to a paid plan. Dont worry, you wont lose any client or session data.

On our Free plan, youll get unlimited notes, but you wont have access to live session capture options, several note types, or more advanced tools. You can still generate notes from written summaries, dictation, and uploaded recordings, plus use Upheal for secure video calls without note generation.

Frequently asked questions

How does Upheal's pricing work?

For individuals, we offer a free plan and two paid tiers at set prices. If youd like to use our free plan, no credit card is required. Each higher tier includes more advanced features. You can pick a monthly plan, or save 30% with a yearly plan.

For group practices, please schedule time with our sales team to get a quote customized to your practice's needs.

Can I customize my plan?

As of now, the options for our individual plans are set. However, if your larger organization would like to join Upheal, we might be able to accommodate more specific needs. Please feel free to reach out to us.

Can I pause my subscription/payment?

You can pause your subscription for up to two months. To pause, contact Support, choose a date to automatically resume your plan, and reach out to confirm when youre ready to start again. While your subscription is paused, you can still sign in to your account and access your notes its just like being on our free plan.

How secure is Upheal?

Upheal is committed to the highest standards of privacy and security. Our platform is HIPAA-compliant and adheres other privacy regulations worldwide. We use record-level encryption and perform regular security audits to protect your and your clients data. More on our privacy practices and technical security monitoring.

How do I switch to an organization-paid plan?

If you use Upheal as an individual, and youre going to be added to a larger practice or organization, the paying organization will invite you to their Team plan. If you want your clients and data transferred to your new organization, please contact Upheal Support.

Will I be charged sales tax?

You wont be charged sales tax on your Upheal subscription.

How and when can I cancel my subscription or trial?

You can cancel any time. Just contact Support, and well assist. When you cancel, your subscription remains active until the end of your current billing period, and then you wont be charged again.

For both monthly and yearly plans, we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee in case you change your mind about your subscription. After 30 days, any cancellation will take effect at the end of your billing cycle, monthly or annual.

Where can I update my payment method?

To update your payment method, go to Settings>Plans and billing. If you need help, please contact Support in the Upheal app, and well assist.
